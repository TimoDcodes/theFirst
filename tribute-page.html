Kakarot (Son Goku)

The Man of Many Colors.

  • Great Ape - Son Goku transforms into a giant ape when he looks at the full moon (Dragon Ball)
  • Super Saiyan - Son Goku's first transformation into a Super Saiyan occurs during his battle with Frieza on planet Namek (Dragon Ball Z)
  • Super Saiyan 2 - Son Goku achieves this form during the Cell Games in order to defeat Cell (Dragon Ball Z)
  • Super Saiyan 3 - Son Goku unlocks this transformation in Other World and uses it to battle Majin Buu (Dragon Ball Z)
  • Super Saiyan God - Son Goku transforms into a Super Saiyan God during the events of Dragon Ball Super
  • Super Saiyan Blue - Son Goku achieves this form by combining his Super Saiyan God form with the Super Saiyan transformation (Dragon Ball Super)
  • Ultra Instinct - Son Goku attains this transformation during the Tournament of Power and is able to fight at an incredible level without thinking (Dragon Ball Super)
